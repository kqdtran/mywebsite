Currently...

Khoa is a junior at University of California, Berkeley where he majors in Computer Science and plans to graduate in May 2014. Currently holding a cumulative UC GPA of 3.59, he is working under Professor Anant Sahai as a Reader/Grader for CS 70 - Discrete Mathematics and Probability Theory - where he graded students' weekly problem sets and held office hours to help them better understand the material. Khoa is also part of the Mathapedia Research Group in the EECS Department, and he wants to help change the future of online education, where course material are open to everyone around the world with an Internet connection and a Javascript-supported browser.

This semester, he's taking CS61B: Data Structures, EE42: Intro to Digital Electronics, IEOR115: Industrial and Commercial Data Systems, and auditting CS170: Algorithms. He really wants to take the last one, but apparently there was no space left and priority went to graduating seniors :(. Feel free to contact him if you happen to be in one of those classes and would like to study together.

In The Future...

In his senior year, Khoa plans to take a lot of upper division courses - mainly because he was a junior transfer and had to catch up with the rest of the Class of 2014, but he also hopes to TA for a course at Berkeley at least once before graduating. His motto is "YOBUO", which stands for "You Only Be Undergrad Once", and Khoa wants to do anything in his ability in his short two years at UC Berkeley.

After graduating, Khoa plans to work as a Software Engineer for a few years, but eventually his long-term goal is to return to Vietnam and help his mother run her startup. He believes that his education in the U.S., along with his entrepreneural spirit, will allow the company to achieve greater growth than ever.
